PolishedGaze by Abhinaya

UX Case Study: Redefining Digital Artistry in Nail Care

UX Research by: Avanthikha

1. Project Overview

PolishedGaze is a boutique nail studio in Coimbatore driven by artistry and technical precision. This case study explores the design of a professional web presence intended to transition the studio from fragmented social media interactions to a streamlined, high-conversion digital platform while preserving the artist’s "human-centric" service philosophy.

2. Business Background

Origin: Abhinaya’s journey was sparked by digital inspiration and a personal revelation regarding the tactile beauty of nail art.
The Brand: Featuring a self-made brand identity, the studio prioritizes advanced technical structures and the use of premium products. Abhinaya’s character is defined by a relentless drive to upgrade her skills, often researching global structures to provide top-tier results.

3. Problem Statement

The reliance on manual booking via Instagram DMs and WhatsApp has created significant friction. Potential clients face uncertainty regarding pricing and delayed responses, while the artist experiences administrative fatigue. Without a central hub, the studio lacks the digital visibility needed to reach a wider audience effectively.

4. Business Goals

  • Develop a centralized booking interface to minimize manual administrative work.
  • Showcase a premium portfolio to validate design quality and pricing tiers.
  • Establish a foundation for future e-commerce growth (Vanity Kits and Professional Products).

5. User Research Summary

Surveys conducted with over 20 participants revealed that while 75% find inspiration on Instagram, 90% find "hidden pricing" to be a major deterrent. Key user expectations include a visual gallery, transparent cost breakdowns, and clear contact information.

6. Target Audience

  • The Bride: Users seeking bespoke, high-reliability designs for weddings.
  • The Student: Trend-conscious individuals seeking the best "vibe" within a specific budget.
  • The Explorer: Users trying nail art for the first time who require education and guidance.

7. Key Insights

  • High-Touch Interaction: The artist prefers personal input over automated bots to ensure each client receives a tailored recommendation.
  • Expert Influence: Only 10% of users are strictly design-specific; the majority look to the artist for guidance, highlighting the need for a "Consultant" profile.

8. Pain Points

  • Users expressed frustration with "complicated booking" and "no design references."
  • A lack of clear time-slot availability leads to back-and-forth messaging that delays confirmation.

9. Opportunities

  • Guided Intake: Using a website form to collect the "vibe" and "age group," allowing the artist to respond with customized inputs as requested.
  • Authority Building: Highlighting the artist's learning journey and technical upgrades to justify premium value.

10. Feature Requirements

  • Current: Visual Portfolio, Tiered Pricing List, Integrated Booking Inquiries.
  • Future: Educational Blog (The Polish Edit) and a Shop for specialized Vanity Kits.

11. Booking System Analysis

The solution is a Smart Redirect System: A website form captures design preferences, budget, and desired time, then bundles this into a pre-filled WhatsApp message. This satisfies the user’s need for speed and the artist’s need for a personalized, non-robotic response.

12. Competitive Context

While local commercial studios focus heavily on aggressive mass-market promotions and high-volume turnover, PolishedGaze distinguishes itself through individual craftsmanship, technical excellence, and a deeply personalized client journey.

13. Constraints

  • Service Duration: High-quality art requires 2–3 hours; the website must manage expectations around "quality over speed."
  • Organic Focus: The artist prioritizes repeat clients over paid ads; the site must leverage SEO and user experience to drive organic growth.
