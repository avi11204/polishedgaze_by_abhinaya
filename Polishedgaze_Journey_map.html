User Journey Map

PolishedGaze by Abhinaya

Stage User Actions Mindset (Thoughts & Emotions) Pain Points UX Opportunities
1. Discovery Browses Instagram Reels/Stories; Clicks "Link in Bio" via mobile. "This art is unique!"
Excited, curious but wary of distance/cost.		 Hard to find pricing; slow DM replies; no immediate "call to action." Direct link to a mobile-friendly site with clear categorizations and "Starting From" rates.
2. Exploration Views Gallery; filters by "Bridal" or "Student Trends"; checks the "About" page. "Can she do my Pinterest design?"
Impressed, analytical, looking for skill markers.		 Overwhelmed by uncategorized photos; doesn't know what "Foreign Structure" means. Categorized visual boards and a "Why quality takes time" educational section.
3. Decision Compares pricing tiers; reads reviews; looks for available slots. "Is it worth the 3-hour trip?"
Reassured but looking for transparency.		 Confusion over "add-on" costs; fear of judgment for being "budget-conscious." Interactive "Build your Set" price calculator to remove "Pricing Anxiety."
4. Booking Fills out "Vibe Check" form; receives a personalized WhatsApp redirect. "I hope I can get Saturday at 10 AM."
Hopeful but impatient for confirmation.		 Waiting for manual DM/WA response; no real-time calendar view. Soft-booking calendar that captures data first, then sends a pre-filled WhatsApp for the "personal touch."
5. Service Undergoes the 2-3 hour procedure; interacts with Abhinaya. "The detail is incredible."
Relaxed, appreciative of the precision.		 Session length can be tiring; forgetting specific aftercare instructions. QR code at the station linking to an "Aftercare Hub" and the studio's blog to read during the session.
6. Post-Service Posts a "Nail-fie" on IG; follows aftercare; books a refill. "I need to tell everyone about her!"
Delighted, proud, loyal.		 Nails breaking due to poor aftercare; forgetting to leave a Google review. Automated "24-hour check-in" message with a review link and referral discount.
