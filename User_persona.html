Professional Background

Abhinaya is an artist who doesn't just "paint nails"—she builds structures. Inspired by digital global trends and solidified by real-world craftsmanship seen in traditional ceremonies, she bridges the gap between modern tech and artisanal service. She is a self-made entrepreneur who values quality over volume.

Motivations & Goals
  • Continuous Upgrading: To master foreign nail structures and bring global quality to Coimbatore.
  • The Human Connection: To ensure every client feels heard through manual, personalized responses rather than automated scripts.
  • Evolution: Expanding from service-based artistry to a product-driven brand (Vanity Kits/Blogs).
Business Philosophy

"I want people to come back for the work itself." Abhinaya believes in a high-touch service model. She isn't afraid of the 4 AM to 9 PM grind because her focus is on creating a legacy of skill and trust, not just a high-turnover studio.

"I want my digital presence to be as user-centric as my physical studio. No bots, no judgment—just a space where I can guide someone to their best 'vibe' while exploring the limits of nail structure and design."
